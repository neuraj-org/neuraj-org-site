🎯 BRAND INTENTION
───────────────────────────────────────────────

precise, calm, and evolving. Every visual element should mirror how 
Neuraj behaves — intelligent, connected, and quietly powerful.

- Momentum

- Evolution
- Purpose
- Calm Power
- Connection

🎨 LOGO IDEATION
───────────────────────────────────────────────
Chosen Style: Hybrid (symbol + wordmark)
Symbol Concept: Geometric “N” formed from neural threads / circuits.
Tone: Minimal, silver on black, institutional.
Fonts considered: Space Grotesk, Montserrat, Qanelas Soft.

Rejected Styles:
- Gradient neon variants (too flashy)
- Rounded friendly fonts (reduced precision)
- Overly complex neuron meshes (too noisy)

Notes:
Keep logo scalable, symmetrical, and easily embeddable into motion graphics.
